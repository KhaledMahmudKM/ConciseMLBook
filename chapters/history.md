# A Brief History of Machine Learning

Machine learning has its roots in statistics, computer science, and neuroscience.

## Early Milestones

- **1950s**: Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test.
- **1957**: Perceptron algorithm introduced by Frank Rosenblatt.
- **1980s–90s**: Rise of expert systems and neural networks.
- **2000s–present**: Explosion in computational power, data availability, and deep learning.

## Key Events

- **1997**: IBM’s Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov.
- **2012**: AlexNet wins the ImageNet challenge, kicking off the deep learning boom.
- **2016**: Google’s AlphaGo defeats a Go world champion.

These breakthroughs laid the foundation for modern ML.
